Mabuhay! I am Claire, an aspiring web developer passionate about programming and crafting meaningful websites and applications that shape the world of technology. I’m also a UI/UX designer and full‑stack developer in progress, with a love for classical music, art, lavender, lily of the valley, and reading the Bible. Beyond tech, I enjoy writing about the beauty of life.


Currently in my 3rd year at Holy Angel University, I pursue my journey in tech with curiosity and purpose. Growing up in a Christian family devoted to ministry has shaped my values and continues to guide the way I approach my work. I find joy in food trips with friends, family, or even solo adventures, and in the simple, meaningful moments of everyday life.

I aspire to become a full‑stack developer and UI/UX designer, crafting purposeful digital experiences that solve real‑world problems with creativity and clarity. I thrive on blending design and code to build interfaces that feel intuitive and inspiring. My mission is to connect people through advancing technology, and my vision is to serve real needs. Inspired by art, music, and storytelling, I weave these influences into the digital space. For me, every project is an opportunity to learn, refine, and create with intention — because I believe in design that resonates and code that empowers.
